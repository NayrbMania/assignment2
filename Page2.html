Overview


We offer programs for complete beginners to pro athletes.
All age groups, genders, skill levels, and fitness levels are welcome.
Oxygen has the best trainers and best programmes in the world.

Hour of Power

HIIT is one of the most popular forms of exercise in the world.
Taking only one short hour, this intense workout will leave you aching for days.
Simple exercises that anyone can do but at an intensity to push you to your limit.
Take this class if you want to feel the burn and lose some body fat.

Cross Fit

Cross Fit is one of the fastest growing sports in the world.
Athletes need to be powerful, fast and fit to succeed.
It has a good mix of cardiovascular fitness and muscular required.
Join now to be a fit and functional all rounder.

Strongman

Strongman is one of the oldest power sports in history dating back to the highland games.
Don't want to look lean and fit? Wanna be strong and powerful? Strongman is the sport for you.
All about power, strongman appeals to those who want inhuman functional strength.
Join us now and unleash your inner beast
